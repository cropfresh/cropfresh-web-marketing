We are excited to announce our official launch in Karnataka!

## Our Mission
To eliminate inefficiencies in the traditional supply chain, ensuring farmers get a larger share of the value while buyers receive fresher, guaranteed quality produce.

## Scale Targets
We aim to onboard 50,000 farmers and establish 200 Village Drop Points across the state by the end of the year.
