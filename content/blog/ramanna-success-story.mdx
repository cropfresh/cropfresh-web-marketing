Ramanna, a tomato farmer in Kolar, used to struggle with unpredictable mandi prices and multiple middlemen taking a cut of his profits.

## Discovering CropFresh
With a simple smartphone photo, Ramanna now gets an instant AI-powered quality assessment and a guaranteed price.

## The Impact
By taking his produce directly to the Village Drop Point, he now averages ₹25/kg compared to the ₹15/kg he used to receive.

"CropFresh has given me certainty. I know exactly what I'll earn before I even load my truck," says Ramanna.
