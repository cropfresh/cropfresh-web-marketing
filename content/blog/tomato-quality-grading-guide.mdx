## Understanding Tomato Grades

Tomatoes are graded based on size, color, firmness, and blemishes.

### Grade A
Perfectly round, uniform color, completely firm, and zero blemishes. These fetch the highest price from premium buyers.

### Grade B
Slight variations in shape or color, minor superficial marks, but otherwise firm and healthy.

### Grade C
Suitable for processing. Irregular shapes, varying ripeness, or noticeable but non-rotting blemishes.
