Crops in South India have always been subject to extreme seasonal volatility.

## Analyzing the Data
Looking at APMC mandi rates over the last decade, we see consistent spikes and crash cycles tied to monsoon patterns and festival demands.

## How DPLE Helps
The Dynamic Price Liquidity Engine (DPLE) acts as a buffer. By analyzing market signals in real-time and establishing direct procurement contracts, CropFresh smooths out to the most extreme dips, ensuring a fair baseline price even during gluts.
